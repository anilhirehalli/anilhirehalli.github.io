What I do

Vulnerability Management

Vulnerability Management is a crucial cybersecurity process involving continuous identification, evaluation, remediation, and reporting of vulnerabilities. It includes regular scanning, maintaining an updated asset inventory, prioritizing vulnerabilities, applying fixes, and using temporary mitigation measures when needed. Effective documentation, communication, and continuous monitoring help enhance security posture, ensure compliance, and reduce exploitation risks.

Red Team

Red Teaming is a cybersecurity exercise where experts simulate real-world attacks on an organization to identify vulnerabilities and test defenses. This practice helps uncover gaps not evident in regular audits, allowing the internal security team to improve their detection and response capabilities, ultimately strengthening the organization's overall security posture.

Technical Talk / Workshop

Technical talks and workshops in cybersecurity are crucial for professional growth. Technical talks offer expert insights on specific topics and trends, while workshops provide hands-on training in areas like penetration testing and network security. Both help professionals stay updated on advancements and best practices, and offer networking opportunities with peers and experts.
